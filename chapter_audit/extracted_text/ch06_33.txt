Comparison: In general, I1 ̸= I2 because e2πi(n+1)/n − 1 ̸= e2πi/n − 1 in general.
Should they agree? No, they should not agree in general. The two paths are not
homotopic in the cut plane. The circular path C1 encloses the origin (the branch point),
while the keyhole path does not (it runs along the cut). On the cut plane (a simply-connected
domain), different paths between the same endpoints give the same integral only if they are
homotopic. The circular path cannot be continuously deformed into the keyhole path without
crossing the branch cut.
However, the two results are related: their difference equals the integral around a closed
contour encircling the branch point.
